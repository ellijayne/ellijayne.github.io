Kana-o-Manabu! (Learn the Hiragana Alphabet)

Kana-o-Manabu means 'Learn Kana' in Japanese. This web app was designed to help people learn the 46 basic kana characters of the Hiragana alphabet.

It all started when I decided to teach myself Japanese! There were a lot of images of the Hiragana characters online but no straight forward apps for me to use, so I thought 'one day when I am a Web Developer I will build a simple app to help people learn Hiragana' and I did just that for my final project!

The app uses mnemonics and short quizzes to help people memorise the characters. Users can type their responses in Hiragana as I have utilised a Hiragana IME JavaScript plug-in to convert their answers from English to Hiragana.

I am very excited to continue working on this app and adding additional features as learning Japanese is a passion of mine!

  • Ruby on Rails ||
